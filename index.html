Why Sanmargaha Exists

We built Sanmargaha to close the daily learning gap for families who need quality education that is close, consistent, and affordable.

The Gap We Solve

  • Students face long travel after school, leading to fatigue and reduced focus.
  • Parents struggle to balance quality, convenience, and affordability.
  • Learning gaps grow when classroom concepts are missed or unclear.
  • One-to-one tuition is expensive and difficult to scale.
  • Many learners miss out on personalized academic attention.

Our Unique Learning Model: Edu Habitat

We bring high-quality coaching into residential communities, making learning simple, accessible, and effective.
