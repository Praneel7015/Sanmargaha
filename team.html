About Sanmargaha

Rooted in Values, Driven by Excellence

Founded in 2025, Sanmargaha was built on a simple belief: each child deserves a guide who cares deeply. Over the year, we have grown from one classroom in Belagavi to a trusted learning network across Karnataka.

Our name reflects our purpose. Sanmargaha means walking the right path. We walk that path with every learner, every family, every day.

Nurture

We cultivate each child's unique strengths and potential.

High standards with patient mentoring at every step.
