About Sanmargaha

Rooted in Values, Driven by Excellence

Founded in 2008, Sanmargaha was built on a simple belief: each child deserves a guide who cares deeply. Over 17 years, we have grown from one classroom in Belagavi to a trusted learning network across Karnataka.

Our name reflects our purpose. Sanmargaha means walking the right path. We walk that path with every learner, every family, every day.
